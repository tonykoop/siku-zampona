# Image Generation Prompt

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 studio product photograph of a contemporary workshop interpretation of the Andean Aymara/Quechua siku or zampona family, focused on the Zanka tenor build target: two complementary arka and ira ranks of bamboo tubes with closed lower ends, longest tubes at the left and shortest at the right, bound with waxed cord into clean cherry or walnut frame rails. Show the stepped tube profile, natural bamboo color, careful beeswax sealing at the bottoms, respectful simple finish, clean neutral background, soft directional studio lighting, high detail, 50mm lens look.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic scene of two players holding complementary arka and ira siku ranks in a warm workshop or small rehearsal space, the instruments clearly separate but visually paired, with natural light, spare wooden benches, tuning notebook, and extra bamboo stock nearby. The image should communicate interlocking duet use and contemporary workshop respect without theatrical costume or cultural caricature.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of the siku construction: bamboo tube mouths aligned flush at the blow end, waxed cord binding over a cherry or walnut rail, a stepped row of closed tubes descending in length, and subtle beeswax plugs visible at the lower ends of adjacent tubes. Use shallow depth of field, natural bamboo fiber texture, and no printed note labels.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, no watermarks, no brand logos, no extra limbs, anatomically plausible hands if shown, no cultural costume stereotypes, no random tube counts, no dimensions, no tuning chart, no toolpaths.
